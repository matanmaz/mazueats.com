MAZU is a Yellow Springs restaurant featuring a menu inspired by our diverse backgrounds, including Taiwanese, Israeli-Palestinian, and other cuisines. We offer a unique dining experience that's not just about the meal, but also about sharing stories and flavors. MAZU was created as a welcoming community space for food, art, activism and collaboration.

We offer a casual dining experience, with counter service and plenty of indoor and outdoor seating. The space is wheelchair accessible and child friendly. Our menu is fully vegan with gluten and allergen free options.

We also host private dinners and events. Come by and eat with us!
